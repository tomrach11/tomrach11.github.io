Restaurant Description

Anime Curry Restaurant is bringing one of the most popular menu on anime to live. The Japanese curry is one of the most iconic menu in Japan and it shows it original and iconic regularly on so many popular anime shows. Anime Curry has variety types of curry menu from plain iconic curry, types of meat your can put on with fries and stew to omelette rice. Our restaurant also has iconic Japanese street food vibe to it, make you feel like you are actually in Japan. Come experience it yourself.
